DigiCup 2020

By Defimedia.info

Competition on Digital solutions - The Digicup: ICT students on stage

In order to acquire the skills sought by employers in the Information and Communication Technology (ICT) sector, Mauritian students put themselves in the position of service providers. To do this, they are currently organizing a competition, where they must produce a digital solution for an association that actually exists. This is how the concept of the Digicup was born. This competition is organized by the Information, Communication & Digital Technologies (ICDT) Society of the University of Mauritius (UoM). The student members of this group are supported by associations in the sector, such as the Outsourcing Telecommunications Association of Mauritius (OTAM), the Mauritius Information Technologies Industry Association (MITIA) and the Chamber of Commerce and Industry France Mauritius (CCIFM) , through the Federation of Innovative & Numeric Activities in Mauritus (FINAM). Companies such as SD Worx, Accenture Technology, AEROW ECM World, Nova Interactions, ICT.io, iQera and Cyberskills are also providing support.

“The DigiCup is also a challenge for the ICDT Society and we have outdone ourselves for this first. We are happy to see that some of our goals are already materializing, as friendships are being forged between the students. A network of computer science students from different universities is gradually forming. During these first two weeks, the candidates participated in extraordinary activities through intensive bootcamps on design thinking, new project management methods such as Scrum and Agile. These are undeniable assets for stimulating creativity or thinking out of the box, learning to optimize resources and our time,” says Irfaan Ali Subratty, President of the ICDT Society.

The participants are divided into teams of four people. They come from UoM, University of the Mascarenes, University of Technology of Mauritius (UTM), Middlesex University, Polytechnics Mauritius and MCCI Business School

My Reflection
